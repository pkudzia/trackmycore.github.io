Our Product

The Core Motion system consists of three key components working together to provide real-time biomechanical feedback.

Wearable Sensor Array

Lightweight, medical-grade IMU sensors that track movement with 0.01cm accuracy.

Processing Unit

Proprietary algorithms that calculate center of mass in real-time at 100Hz.

Mobile App

Visual feedback, session tracking, and performance analytics.
