Our Story

At Core Motion, the vision is clear: to make advanced biomechanical insight accessible to athletes and clinicians everywhere. The founders—experienced in both sports medicine and cutting-edge sensor technology—saw firsthand the impact of subjective assessment and prohibitive lab costs.

Driven by this challenge, they set out to build a new standard: a wearable platform delivering research-grade precision in a portable, affordable form. Today, Core Motion bridges the gap between innovation and daily practice, empowering users to make confident, evidence-based decisions and helping athletes safely reach their highest potential.

Our Mission

Core Motion exists to make precise biomechanical analysis accessible to every athlete and clinician. By combining advanced sensor technology with intuitive, affordable solutions, we empower individuals to track movement objectively, optimize rehabilitation, and make confident, data-driven return-to-play decisions—anywhere.
