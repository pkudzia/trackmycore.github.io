Our Story

Core Motion was born in Vancouver, Canada, from a simple but powerful observation: athletes recovering from ACL injuries often regained their strength and fitness, yet movement quality remained compromised. Diego, a sport physiotherapist and on-field specialist who has worked with over 500 athletes, kept seeing the same pattern. Athletes met traditional return-to-sport criteria but still demonstrated movement patterns that increased re-injury risk.

Through years of clinical experience and evidence-based practice, Diego recognized that movement compensations, whether at the knee or anywhere else, consistently traced back to trunk control and core stability.

That's when Diego met Dr. Pawel Kudzia, a biomechanist and engineer who was equally passionate about solving this problem. Pawel had spent years developing advanced sensor systems and understood exactly why lab-grade movement analysis was stuck in expensive labs instead of being in the hands of the therapists and athletes who needed it most.

Together, they had a breakthrough idea: what if we could bring precise biomechanical measurement out of the lab and onto the field? What if therapists could see exactly how their athletes were moving, in real time, and guide them to move better?

Core Motion was born from that vision. By combining Diego's clinical expertise with Pawel's engineering innovation, they're building technology that makes objective movement analysis accessible, portable, and practical for everyone.

Today, with support and mentorship from Venture UBC, Core Motion is transforming how athletes recover and return to sport. We're empowering physiotherapists to bridge the gap between the clinic and the field, giving them the tools to help athletes train and recover where it matters most: on the court, the field, or the rink.

This is just the beginning of our journey, and we're excited to have you join us as we work to reduce re-injury rates and help athletes reach their full potential.

Our Mission

Core Motion exists to make precise biomechanical analysis accessible to every athlete and clinician. By combining advanced sensor technology with intuitive, affordable solutions, we empower individuals to track movement objectively, optimize rehabilitation, and make confident, data-driven return-to-play decisions—anywhere.
