Our Story

Core Motion was founded in 2023 by Diego Grossling and Dr. Pawel Kudzia with a shared vision to democratize biomechanical analysis for athletes of all levels.

After years working in sports medicine and rehabilitation, Diego recognized the gap between clinical-grade motion analysis and what was available to everyday athletes. Meanwhile, Pawel was developing novel sensor fusion algorithms during his PhD research.

Combining their expertise, they created the first wearable system that tracks center of mass with research-grade accuracy at a fraction of the cost of existing solutions.
