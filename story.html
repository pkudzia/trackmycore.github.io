Our Story

At Core Motion, we envision a future where every athlete and clinician can access advanced biomechanical insights—no matter where they train or practice. We believe that empowering people with meaningful movement data will transform rehabilitation, athletic performance, and injury prevention.

Our founding team brings together deep expertise in sports medicine and sensor technology. Witnessing the limits of subjective assessment and costly laboratory equipment, they recognized the urgent need for practical, objective solutions available beyond research facilities and elite clinics.

The result is a wearable system that makes research-grade biomechanics both portable and affordable, enabling precise motion tracking and data-driven decisions for clinicians and athletes everywhere.

Our Mission

Core Motion exists to make precise biomechanical analysis accessible to every athlete and clinician. By combining advanced sensor technology with intuitive, affordable solutions, we empower individuals to track movement objectively, optimize rehabilitation, and make confident, data-driven return-to-play decisions—anywhere.
